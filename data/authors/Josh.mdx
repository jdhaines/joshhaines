Josh Haines is a **Software Specialist** and **Senior Materials Engineer** with 16+ years of experience solving problems in complex industries. He is a Lilly Scholar who worked for Honeywell Aerospace and Johns Manville before rejoining Rolls-Royce ten years after an internship. In 2020, he transitioned to a full-time role in the Digital Transformation team and started a Software Factory to enable Rolls-Royce to develop modern software applications and services. In late 2024, he was promoted to **VP Digital Products** to lead multiple digital product delivery teams within the larger Digital and IT umbrella.

As a three-time small business owner and recovering serial hobbyist, he loves to learn new skills and is passionate about lifelong learning. He incorporates his self-taught abilities as a developer, skills coach, and DevSecOps enthusiast into projects both professional and charitable.

Josh lives with his wife Chris and daughters Jenny and Nora. He holds a BS and MS from Purdue University in Materials Engineering.
